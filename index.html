Haikyuu!!

Haikyu!! is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate. Individual chapters have been serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump since February 2012. The series was initially published as a one-shot in Shueisha's seasonal Jump NEXT! magazine prior to serialization.
